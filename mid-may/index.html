I Cannot Stop Myself From Buying Records

Hailu Mergia, Todd Terry, Bella Boo

Hailu Mergia
Lalu Belu Jaunty Ethio-jazz with a rotating cast of lead voices - piano, organ, synth, and accordion get alternating, sometimes overlapping, takes at steering the close-knit, raucous rhythm section. The party concludes abruptly with a stumbling ballad on solo piano that winds the album to a bummer conclusion.

Toddy Terry
Remixes Nothing more or less than a mix of alt-Jock Jams, a deviation that seeks heart before ass, or at least regards them as equivalent partners on the dance floor. His emphatic hi-hats and midrange bass tend to confine his palette from the remotely soulful. The dominant value here is his skill at rearrangment, restructuring pop songs into house music narratives.

Bella Boo
Fire WOw! Beatific and dazed house music mostly, with some downtempo stirred in, craftily tacked together from upcycled samples and light-footed drum programming. Handcrafted for those deep feels, like if Etsy had a record label.
