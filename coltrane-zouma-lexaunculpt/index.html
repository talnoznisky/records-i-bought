I Cannot Stop Myself From Buying Records

John Coltrane, Yumi Zouma, and Lexaunculpt

John Coltrane
Lush Life
I love Coltrane. His soloing pulls so far away from the relatively staid rhythm section. Occasionally they pull together some syncopated phrasing too.

Yumi Zouma
EP III
Majestic 80s power pop sounds massaged into some beautiful love songs. I played this so many times off Spotify that I realized it's an important record to me. It's very touching and danceable and warm.

Lexaunculpt
Oh Here's Some Noises
Oh shit I may already have a copy of this record. I was listening passively to side A because it was just some crunchy IDM beats and then a really emotional set of strings and noises got my attention. It's really devoted to weird sounds and emotions when it wants to be. It's from 1998!
